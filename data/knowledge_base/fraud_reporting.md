# Fraud Reporting and Disputes

## Reporting Suspected Fraud

If you suspect unauthorized activity on your account, contact us
immediately at 1-888-PNC-BANK (1-888-762-2265). Fraud reporting is
available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

For debit card fraud, you may also lock your card immediately through
the mobile app under Card Controls. This prevents further transactions
without canceling your account.

## Federal Protections

Under Regulation E, you have the following protections for unauthorized
electronic fund transfers from your account:

- Report within 2 business days of discovery: maximum liability $50
- Report within 60 days of statement: maximum liability $500
- Report after 60 days: you may be liable for all losses from
  unauthorized transactions

Credit card fraud is governed by Regulation Z, which limits your
liability to $50 regardless of reporting timeline.

## Investigation Timeline

Once you report fraud, we will:

1. Issue provisional credit within 10 business days if the investigation
   cannot be completed in that time (Regulation E requirement).
2. Complete the investigation within 45 days for most claims, or 90
   days for new accounts (less than 30 days old) or international
   transactions.
3. Notify you in writing of the investigation outcome.

## Dispute Documentation

For best results, provide:

- Date and amount of the disputed transaction
- Merchant name as it appears on your statement
- Reason for the dispute (unauthorized, duplicate, did not receive
  goods, etc.)
- Any supporting documentation (receipts, correspondence with merchant)

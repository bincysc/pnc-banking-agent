# Personal Account Types and Features

## Checking Accounts

**Performance Select Checking** — Our standard checking account.
$5 monthly maintenance fee, waived with $1,500 minimum daily balance or
$2,000 in monthly direct deposits. Includes free online banking, mobile
deposit, and bill pay. Standard overdraft protection applies.

**Virtual Wallet** — A bundle of checking, short-term savings, and
long-term savings accounts. $7 monthly fee, waived with $500 minimum
balance across all three accounts. Includes Calendar payment tracking
and Money Bar visualization tools.

**Performance Spend Checking** — Premium tier checking. $25 monthly
fee, waived with $5,000 minimum daily balance. Includes ATM fee
reimbursement (up to $20/month), free standard checks, and priority
phone support.

## Savings Accounts

**Standard Savings** — 0.01% APY. $5 monthly maintenance fee, waived
with $300 minimum daily balance. Six withdrawals per month allowed
under Federal Reserve Regulation D.

**High Yield Savings** — 4.25% APY (variable, reviewed monthly). No
monthly fee with $1 minimum opening deposit. Six withdrawals per month
allowed under Regulation D.

## Credit Cards

**Cash Rewards Visa** — 2% cash back on all purchases, no annual fee.
**Travel Rewards Visa** — 3x points on travel and dining, $95 annual fee.
**Secured Visa** — Build credit with a refundable security deposit.

## Account Opening Requirements

All accounts require government-issued photo ID, Social Security number,
and proof of address. Minimum opening deposit: $25 for checking, $25
for savings, $0 for credit cards.

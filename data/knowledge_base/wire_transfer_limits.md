# Wire Transfer Limits and Procedures

## Daily Limits

Personal banking customers may initiate domestic wire transfers up to
$25,000 per business day through online banking. Wires above this
threshold require initiation through a banker at a branch location or
through the dedicated wire desk at 1-800-555-0142.

International wire transfers are limited to $10,000 per business day
through online banking. International wires above $10,000 require
in-person verification due to enhanced compliance requirements under
the Bank Secrecy Act.

## Cutoff Times

Domestic wires initiated before 5:00 PM Eastern Time on a business day
are processed same-day. Wires initiated after 5:00 PM ET are processed
the next business day. International wires must be initiated before
4:00 PM ET for same-day processing.

## Fees

Outgoing domestic wires: $30 per transfer for personal accounts.
Outgoing international wires: $45 per transfer for personal accounts.
Incoming wires (domestic and international): $15 per transfer.

Wire fees are waived for Premier Banking customers (account balance
above $100,000) and Private Banking customers.

## Cancellation

Wires may be canceled until the moment of execution. After execution,
recall is possible but not guaranteed and may incur a $35 recall fee.

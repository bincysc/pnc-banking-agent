# Mobile Check Deposit

## Eligibility

Mobile deposit is available to all checking and savings account holders
with an active mobile banking app. Accounts must be open for at least
30 days before mobile deposit is enabled.

## Deposit Limits

Standard limits:
- Daily deposit limit: $5,000
- 30-day rolling deposit limit: $15,000

Premier Banking customers have enhanced limits:
- Daily deposit limit: $25,000
- 30-day rolling deposit limit: $100,000

## How To Deposit

1. Open the mobile app and tap "Deposit"
2. Select the account to deposit into
3. Enter the check amount
4. Photograph the front of the check
5. Endorse the back: signature, "For mobile deposit only at PNC", and
   the last 4 digits of the account number
6. Photograph the back of the check
7. Review and submit

## Funds Availability

The first $225 of a mobile deposit is typically available the same
business day if deposited before 9:00 PM ET. The remainder is available
on the next business day.

For amounts above $5,000 or for new accounts, a longer hold may apply —
up to 9 business days under Regulation CC.

## Check Retention

Keep the original paper check for 14 days after a successful mobile
deposit. After confirming the deposit has posted, mark the check
"VOID — Mobile Deposited" and destroy securely. Do not deposit the
paper check at a branch or ATM.

## What Checks Cannot Be Deposited

- Checks payable to someone other than the account holder
- Checks dated more than 6 months ago
- Foreign checks (checks drawn on banks outside the United States)
- Cashier's checks above $10,000 (deposit at branch instead)
- Money orders

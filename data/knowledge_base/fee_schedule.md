# Fee Schedule

## Monthly Maintenance Fees

| Account Type | Monthly Fee | Waiver Criteria |
|--------------|-------------|------------------|
| Performance Select Checking | $5 | $1,500 daily balance OR $2,000 monthly deposits |
| Virtual Wallet | $7 | $500 combined balance |
| Performance Spend Checking | $25 | $5,000 daily balance |
| Standard Savings | $5 | $300 daily balance |
| High Yield Savings | $0 | Always free |

## Transaction Fees

ATM withdrawals at PNC ATMs: Free
ATM withdrawals at non-PNC ATMs: $3.00 per transaction (plus operator fees)
Overdraft fee: $36 per item, maximum 4 per day
Returned item fee: $36 per item
Stop payment fee: $33 per request

## Wire Transfer Fees

See the Wire Transfer Limits and Procedures document for full details.
Domestic outgoing: $30. International outgoing: $45. Incoming: $15.

## Account Closure

No fee to close an account. Accounts closed within 180 days of opening
may forfeit any bonus offers received at opening.

## Foreign Transaction Fees

3% foreign transaction fee on debit card purchases outside the United
States. Credit card foreign transaction fees vary by card — Travel
Rewards Visa has no foreign transaction fee.
